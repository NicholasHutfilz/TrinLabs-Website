TrinSpirit

Tiger House Management

TrinSpirit is the home of the Tiger House system, and provides a simple method for students to check which house is in the lead during the annual competition.

Sticker Tag

Trinity's Iconic Game

Sticker Tag is well loved by Trinity Students, and it wouldn't be possible without the Sticker Tag website. It handles the management of the entire game, and displays a public leaderboard with the top players.

  • Link: https://trinitystickertag.com
  • Created By: Calvin '18 and Jake '18

TrinCooks

The Trinity Cookbook

This community cookbook was created to bring the Trinity community together through food. The Trinity Community Cookbook is maintained by TrinLabs and is a special collaboration with the Parent's Association.

  • Link: https://trincooks.com
  • Created By: Alan Hornos-Kohl'22, Ari Sussman '22, Brian Shi '22, Bunjee Awoyomi '22, Eli Rapport '22, Frances Cohen '21, Musa Jallow '22, Philip Pike-Acosta '21, Ryan McGranahan '22, Taran Ramchandani '22, Ursula Parker '21
